S3Model Has Evolved

Now: Semantic Data Charter™

Thank you for visiting. S3Model has been rebranded and significantly enhanced as the Semantic Data Charter (SDC4).

What's Changed?

The Semantic Data Charter represents the evolution of S3Model into a comprehensive, standards-based specification for semantic data modeling, built on 16+ international standards from W3C, ISO, and IETF.

For readers of our peer-reviewed publications: All S3Model research, concepts, and specifications have been integrated into the Semantic Data Charter with enhanced capabilities and broader standards compliance.
